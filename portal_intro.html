AVS Live Portal

This portal is a live video and audio feed to an Amazon Echo Show 5.
You can ask it questions or to preform a "skill".

How To Use The Portal

- Wear headphones or earbuds if you can.
- When you enter the portal, make sure that your mic is off
- Once you're in, type your name in the chat (bottom left corner) to create an order of who will ask a question.
- Wait you turn, make sure your mic is off when you're not speaking, and don't talk over anyone who is asking a question.
- When your mic is open, try to avoid extranous sounds (key clicks, mouse clicks, etc).
- Speak clearly into the mic.
- When it is your turn to speak, start your senternce with "Alexa" or "Hey Alexa".
- Then ask a question or give a command, starting with a words such "How," "Why," "Play," "Find," etc.
- When you're done speaking. Mute your mic.
