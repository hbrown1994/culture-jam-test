Process Documentation

My initial idea for Alexa Voice Scramble was to play back scrambled vocal recordings to an Alexa. Having some peripheral experience using an Alexa, I knew that I would have to incorporate a “Wake Word” into my system. A wake word is what the Alexa listens for 24/7 in order to begin a query to its servers to listen to the user’s request to generate a response. By default, the wake word is Alexa, so I stuck with that.


Attempt 1: Engage Word Failure

In the first test with the system, the user would record their question always preceded by a wake word. My Alexa Voice Scramble algorithm would then slice their recording into each individual words and store each word into a separate audio file. This way, the algorithm could retrieve and playback the wake word “Alexa” to the system. The rest of the words in the question would be played back in a random order.
